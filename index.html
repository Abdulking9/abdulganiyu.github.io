Biography

I am an international student from Ghana with a background in Computer Science. My undergraduate research focused on designing and implementing a secure, usable, and scalable incident reporting platform for the Akenten Appiah-Menka University of Skills Training and Entrepreneurial Development (AAMUSTED). My long-term goal is to build trustworthy, AI-powered and web-based systems that can be deployed in African higher education institutions and smart communities.

I have been admitted to multiple graduate programs (Wayne State University – CS; University of Michigan–Flint – CSIS) and I am working to strengthen my research profile with projects in web security, AI content tools, and collaborative research with faculty members such as Prof. Yi Zhu (autonomous driving security, CPS/IoT, adversarial sensing).

Research

[UG Research] A Web-Based Incident Reporting System for AAMUSTED

Abdul Ganiyu Abdul Latif

This project developed a web platform that enables students, staff, and security officers to digitally submit, track, and analyze campus security incidents. The system supports role-based access, evidence upload, and real-time tracking of case status to improve campus safety and administrative visibility.

Technologies: PHP / MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, basic authentication, database design, usability testing.

Chat-Based Learning Assistant for Computer Science Students (Prototype)

Abdul Ganiyu Abdul Latif

A conversational assistant built to help undergraduates query course materials, past questions, and programming labs. The system was experimented with OpenAI-style LLM APIs and Streamlit as a simple frontend.

Tools: Python, Streamlit, OpenAI API.

Crime / Incident Data Prediction for University Campuses (Experimental)

Abdul Ganiyu Abdul Latif

Exploratory work on using historical incident data from a campus reporting platform to forecast peak periods, common incident types, and resource allocation needs. The aim was to integrate this with the reporting system for proactive alerts.

Tools: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn.
